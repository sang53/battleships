How to Play

Battleship is a two-player board game where players try to guess the location of the opponent's warships and sink them by firing shots into their board.
In single player, you will always be Player 1.
In multiplayer, the game will alert which player's turn it is.
The opponent must not look at the screen until it is their turn.
Each player places ships on a 10x10 grid, keeping their ships' locations secret from their opponent. The ships can be placed horizontally or vertically.
Players take turns selecting shots to fire onto their opponents board, which are labelled as hits or misses after firing.
